Here are some answers to common questions about our student attendance tracking system:

How does the facial recognition system work?

The facial recognition system uses advanced algorithms to detect and recognize individual faces in real-time. It captures an image of the student's face and compares it to the images stored in the database to identify the student and mark their attendance.

Is the facial recognition system accurate?

Yes, the facial recognition system is highly accurate and can recognize faces even in low-light conditions. However, in case of any discrepancies, teachers can manually verify the attendance.

How can teachers and administrators access attendance reports?

Teachers and administrators can access attendance reports through the reports pages. The reports provide detailed information about attendance statistics.

What if there is a technical issue with the facial recognition system?

In case of any technical issues, students and teachers can contact technical support through the help and support page. The technical support team will work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Is the facial recognition system compliant with privacy laws?

Yes, the facial recognition system is compliant with all relevant privacy laws and regulations. It is designed to protect student data and privacy, and only authorized personnel can access the data.
